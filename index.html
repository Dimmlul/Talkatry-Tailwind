Psychiatry vs Therapy

Psychiatry and therapy both offer essential support, but they serve different purposes. While psychiatrists focus on diagnosing and treating mental disorders with medical interventions, therapists provide coping strategies and emotional support through talk therapy.

Not sure exactly what you need? One of our psychiatrists can help you with a treatment plan tailored to your unique situation.
